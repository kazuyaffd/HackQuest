#  OSINT Challenge — Golding’s Force

## Challenge
Golding's force will thwart your approach at sea, preventing any advance toward their shores.  
A squad of six stands watch over a digital asset, identifiable by an ID commencing with `317...`.  
What is this asset?


## Step-by-Step Solution

1. **Interpret the clue:**
   - “Golding’s force” → Refers to the **Royal New Zealand Navy**, since *David Proctor Golding* has been connected as a senior naval figure.
   - “Thwart your approach at sea” → Reinforces the **naval/military context**.

2. **Locate the digital asset:**
   - The clue mentions a **digital asset** with an ID starting with `317...`.  
   - This strongly suggests a **Facebook Page ID**.

3. **Search the official Royal New Zealand Navy presence:**
   - Navigate to the official **Royal New Zealand Navy Facebook Page**:  
     [https://www.facebook.com/NZNavy/about_profile_transparency](https://www.facebook.com/NZNavy/about_profile_transparency)

4. **Check Page Transparency:**
   - In the “Page Transparency” section, Facebook reveals the **Page ID**.

5. **Identify the correct asset:**
   - The Page ID is clearly listed as:

     ```
     317342038008
     ```


## What You Learned
- Facebook pages have **unique numeric IDs** visible under *Page Transparency*.  
- Clues can combine **people’s names (Golding)**, **domains (navy/sea)**, and **metadata (page IDs)**.  
- Open-source investigation often requires combining **logic + verification** on official pages.
