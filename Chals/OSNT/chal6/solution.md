# OSINT Challenge - Pentagon on a Lake

## Challenge
> *“In a non-existent state lies a freshwater lake, with a pentagon-shaped entity at its heart. Can you pinpoint the coordinates of this place (entity)?”*


## Step-by-Step Solution

1. **Interpret the riddle**
   - *“Non-existent state”* → Common OSINT phrasing for a polity that some actors dispute or don’t recognize.  
     In this context, the intended answer aligns with **Israel** (per the challenge author’s clueing).
   - *“Freshwater lake”* in Israel → The **Sea of Galilee** (a.k.a. **Lake Kinneret**).

2. **Search & verify**
   - Use Google Search/Maps/Earth:  
     `Sea of Galilee pentagon structure`, `Lake Kinneret floating platform`, or simply zoom into the **center** of the lake on **Satellite** view.
   - Near the center, you’ll spot a **pentagon-like arrangement** around a floating/anchored platform (five markers/lights with a central object).

3. **Read the coordinates**
   - Click the structure in Google Maps/Earth and copy the coordinates shown for the **entity itself** (not the lake centroid).
   - Example DMS read-out seen on Maps:  
     `32°49'15.9"N 35°35'28.5"E`

4. **Convert to Decimal Degrees (DD)**
   - 32°49'15.9"N → **32.821083°**  
   - 35°35'28.5"E → **35.591250°**


## What You Learned
- Interpreting riddle wording to narrow geography (“non-existent state” → Israel).
- Using **Google Maps/Earth Satellite** to visually validate landmarks.
- Converting **DMS → Decimal Degrees** for precise coordinate flags.
