# OSINT Challenge — Kyoto Bike Shop

## Challenge
The bikes I saw in this shop in Kyoto look beautiful! I’d love to know where to buy one.  
Can you find me the phone number of this exact store?


## Step-by-Step Solution

1. **Zoom in on the provided image:**
   - Careful inspection shows a sign with the name **“Alex Moulton”**.

2. **Initial search:**
   - Searching Google for **“Alex Moulton”** leads to the **Moulton Bicycle Company**, a well-known British brand.

3. **Refine the query with location:**
   - Searching for **“Kyoto Alex Moulton”** points to a shop called **Moku2+4** in Kyoto, which sells Moulton bicycles.

4. **Verification:**
   - On the official **Moku2+4** (Kyoto Moulton dealer) website and photos, you can find the same showroom interior as in the challenge image.

5. **Find the contact information:**
   - On their website’s **Sales/Contact** page, the phone number for the shop is listed.

## What You Learned
- **Image detail analysis**: zoom in to extract names, signs, or text.  
- **Pivot searching**: combine brand + location keywords (e.g., “Kyoto Alex Moulton”).  
- **Cross-verification**: confirm via official websites and matching images.  
- **Flags can have multiple valid formats** when phone numbers are involved (local, international).
