## Challenge
Riddle: Add digits of 2025 to get X. Multiply X by 3 to get Y. Flag is `CTF{Y}`.

## Steps
1. Sum digits: 2 + 0 + 2 + 5 = 9
    So X = 9.
2. Multiply by 3: Y = 9 * 3 = 27

## Flag
```

```

## What you learned
- Break the riddle into small arithmetic steps.
- Always re-check sums to avoid simple mistakes.
